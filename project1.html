Here's how to make biscuits and gravy!

A quick and easy meal for anytime of the day

This recipe should take less than 30 minutes and should feed 3-4 people!

To start with, you will need an oven, a pan and something to stir the gravy with


Here is the ingredients list
  • Jimmy Dean's sage sausage
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Salt and Pepper
Here are the steps to making your gravy
  1. Cook sausage on medium to high heat
  2. After sausage is browned, add 1/4 cup of flower and stir until well mixed
  3. Slowly add milk up to a 1/4 to 1/2 gallon
  4. Salt and Pepper to taste
